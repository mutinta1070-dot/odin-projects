Description

These delicious tacos feature warm tortillas filled with savory, seasoned meat and topped with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, cheese, and your favorite sauces.

Theyâ€™re quick to make, full of vibrant flavors, and ideal for an easy, customizable meal.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Onions (optional)
  • Avocado or guacamole (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in a pan and season it with taco seasoning.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  3. Fill each tortilla with the cooked meat.
  4. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and other toppings.
  5. Finish with salsa or sour cream.
  6. Serve immediately.
