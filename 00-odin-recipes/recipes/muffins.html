Description

These soft, fluffy muffins are made with a lightly sweet batter that bakes into a tender crumb and golden top.

Perfect for breakfast or a quick treat, they offer a warm, homemade flavor thatâ€™s comforting and versatile enough for any mix-ins you love.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter (melted)
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and line a muffin tray with paper liners.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Whisk the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Combine wet and dry mixtures gently until just blended.
  5. Fill the muffin cups about two-thirds full.
  6. Bake until golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving.
