Description

This classic burger brings together a juicy, seasoned beef patty layered with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and tangy pickles, all tucked inside a toasted bun.

Itâ€™s simple, satisfying, and packed with comforting flavors that never go out of style

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Season and shape the ground beef into patties.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or pan until done to your liking.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  4. Place the cooked patty on the bun and top with cheese.
  5. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  6. Spread ketchup, mustard, or mayo as desired.
  7. Close the burger and serve warm.
