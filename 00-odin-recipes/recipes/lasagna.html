Description

This classic lasagna recipe layers tender pasta sheets with a rich, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and plenty of melted mozzarella.

Slowly baked until golden and bubbling, it delivers the perfect balance of comfort and flavor in every bite.

Whether for family dinners or special occasions, this lasagna is hearty, satisfying, and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (12–16 sheets, cooked or oven-ready)
  • Ground beef (450–500 g / 1 lb)
  • Olive oil (1–2 tbsp)
  • Onion, finely chopped (1 medium)
  • Garlic, minced (2–3 cloves)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes (3–4 cups)
  • Tomato paste (2 tbsp, optional for richness)
  • Italian seasoning (1–2 tsp)
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Ricotta cheese (1 ½ cups)
  • Egg (1, to bind the ricotta)
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded (2–3 cups)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (½–1 cup)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Make the sauce: Sauté onion and garlic, brown the beef, add tomato sauce and seasoning, and let it simmer.
  2. Mix the ricotta: Combine ricotta, egg, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  3. Cook noodles: Boil lasagna noodles unless using oven-ready.
  4. Assemble: Layer sauce → noodles → ricotta → mozzarella. Repeat and finish with sauce and cheese.
  5. Bake: Cover and bake at 180°C / 350°F for 25–30 minutes, then uncover and bake 10–15 minutes until golden.
  6. Rest: Let it sit for 10–15 minutes before serving.
